Welcome to the WTW CubeSat Club's website!

Our mission at the W.T. Woodson CubeSat Club is to enhance and further our knowledge of outer space, and to provide others with opportunities to assist with our goal of space exploration. We commit ourselves to the values of respect, responsibility, teamwork, collaboration, and integrity through the means of our actions, words, and work ethic. We seek to inquire about innovative ideas to share amongst others; and that we can utilize this gained knowledge to benefit our awareness of the miraculous world that encompasses us.
